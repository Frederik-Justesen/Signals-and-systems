Exercise 12.2

$\textbf{Question}:\\$ Compute the corresponding values of $\beta$ if $\alpha = 0.9, 0.99, 0.999$.

$\textbf{Answer}:\\$ Recall that $\beta = \frac{\alpha}{1-\alpha}$ $$\begin{align} \alpha &= 0.9: \quad \beta = \frac{0.9}{0.1} = 9 \\\\ \alpha &= 0.99: \quad \beta = \frac{0.99}{0.01} = 99 \\\\ \alpha &= 0.999: \quad \beta = \frac{0.999}{0.001} = 999 \\\\\end{align} $$
